American Express

The American Express Company (AmEx) is a multinational financial services corporation headquartered at 200 Vesey Street in the Battery Park City neighborhood of Lower Manhattan in New York City. The company was founded in 1850 and is one of the 30 components of the Dow Jones Industrial Average. The company's logo, adopted in 1958, is a gladiator or centurion whose image appears on the company's well-known traveler's cheques, charge cards, and credit cards.

Project Overview

AMEX customers can login to their AMEX account to buy, renew and cancel credit and other financial cards. They can compare different card options and apply coupons to get a discount. This dashboard is written in React/Redux. Other technologies used are NodeJS, AEM and MySQL server.

I was responsible for managing a team of 5 developers. My roles and responsibilities includes; gathering business requirements, enabling seamless communication between developers and business, creating application performance matrix for data visualization for business, suggesting enhancements and creating frontend task and setting deadlines for the same.
