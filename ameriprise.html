Ameriprise Investment Portfolio Dashboard

Ameriprise Financial, Inc. is a diversified financial services company and bank holding company incorporated in Delaware and headquartered in Minneapolis, Minnesota. It provides financial planning products and services, including wealth management, asset management, insurance, annuities, and estate planning.

Project Overview

Ameriprise customers can login to their account to view, renew and cancel their inverstment products (if applicable). This dashboard is completely written in React/Redux. I was responsible for developing/maintaining the dashboard in a 9 developer team during my tenure at Cognizant. Some of the technologies we used are; React/Redux, Immer, Webpack, Java Spring MVC, AEM and many more.

Notable milestones in the projects were; complete UI revamp with transition from Angular to React, secure monetory transaction implementations, new authorization/authentication flow and major code optimizations.
